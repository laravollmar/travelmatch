The Idea

TravelMatch is a digital platform that combines destination recommendations with social matching. Users answer questions about their travel style, budget, interests and preferences. Based on this, the app suggests suitable destinations and matches users with people planning similar journeys.

Why it matters

Many young travellers want to explore the world but feel overwhelmed by too many options or hesitate to travel alone.

TravelMatch solves this by making travel planning easier, more personal and more connected. For stakeholders, the concept offers potential through partnerships with travel agencies, accommodation providers, tourism boards and lifestyle brands.
